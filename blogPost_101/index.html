Charities

Sept 27th, 2019

GFT provides a platform for consumers to research and donate the cost of an exclusive song provided by artist's to a top rated charity of their choice. All charities have been reviewed by GFT through the top 3 charity watchdog sites; Charity Navigator, Charity Watch, and BBB Wise Giving Alliance. Under the Charities tab on GiveForTunes.org, consumers will find mission statments with a direct link to the charities website along with links to the watchdog sites so that they can review, research, and feel confident their donation is going to use for a proper cause. Artist

Sept 27th, 2019

Under the "Artist" page, consumers will be able to click on an artist to view their bio and listen to a clips of the exclusive songs the artist has donated to GFT. Once the consumer chooses the song they would like to purchase, a drop down box will appear for them to select a top rated charity of their choice to donate the cost of the song after operational costs have been deducted .
